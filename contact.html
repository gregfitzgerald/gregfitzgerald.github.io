Research Collaboration

I'm open to collaboration on projects related to:

  • Meta-analysis and systematic review projects in neuroscience or psychology
  • Research methodology and reproducibility initiatives
  • Alzheimer's disease and aging research
  • Translational validity in preclinical research

If you're working on a meta-analysis or systematic review and would like consultation on methodology, statistical analysis, or study design, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Manuscript Review

I'm happy to provide feedback on drafts related to research methodology, meta-analysis, or neuroscience research. Please include:

  • A brief description of your research question
  • What specific type of feedback you're looking for
  • Your timeline for submission

Note: I typically limit manuscript reviews to 5,000 words and aim to provide feedback within 2-3 weeks.

Response Time

I generally respond to emails within 3-5 business days. If you haven't heard back within a week, feel free to send a follow-up — sometimes messages get lost in the shuffle!
