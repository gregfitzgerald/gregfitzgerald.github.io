Research Tutoring & Statistical Consulting

About the Service

I provide one-on-one tutoring in statistics, research methods, and meta-analysis for undergraduate and graduate students. My approach focuses on building deep conceptual understanding rather than memorizing formulas. Whether you're struggling with hypothesis testing, planning your thesis research, or analyzing data for publication, I can help you develop the skills and confidence you need.

My background includes years of teaching statistics at the undergraduate level, conducting meta-analyses, and mentoring students through research projects. I emphasize understanding the logic behind statistical procedures, interpreting results correctly, and avoiding common pitfalls in research design.

Topics Covered

  • Hypothesis testing, p-values, and statistical significance
  • Effect sizes and confidence intervals
  • Meta-analysis methodology and interpretation
  • Power analysis and sample size planning
  • Common statistical tests (t-tests, ANOVA, regression, chi-square)
  • Research design and methodology
  • Data analysis with R and Python
  • Scientific writing and communication
  • Critical evaluation of research literature
  • Publication bias and questionable research practices
