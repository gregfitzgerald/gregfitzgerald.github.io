Meta-Analysis of Environmental Enrichment in Alzheimer's Disease Models

Research In Progress 2023-2025

Systematic review and meta-analysis examining the effectiveness of environmental enrichment interventions in preclinical models of Alzheimer's disease. This work forms the core of my doctoral dissertation and aims to quantify the efficacy of environmental enrichment across multiple cognitive and neurobiological outcome measures.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive systematic literature search across multiple databases
  • Meta-analytic statistical techniques using R (metafor package)
  • Publication bias assessment and sensitivity analyses
  • Data visualization for scientific communication
  • Investigation of moderator variables affecting treatment efficacy

AI-Resistant Learning Platform

Education Concept 2024

Educational software designed to promote deep learning and critical thinking in an era of AI assistance. The platform uses process-oriented assessment and interactive problem-solving to ensure students develop genuine understanding rather than relying on AI-generated answers.

Core Principles

  • Process-oriented assessment that values problem-solving steps over final answers
  • Interactive simulations requiring real-time decision-making
  • Randomized problem sets preventing answer key dependence
  • Collaborative learning features encouraging peer discussion
  • Focus on conceptual understanding and methodology

Exerkine Performance

Biotech Startup Concept 2024

Biotech startup concept focused on translating exercise-induced signaling molecules (exerkines) into therapeutic interventions. The company aims to develop pharmaceutical approaches to deliver the cognitive and metabolic benefits of exercise to populations unable to engage in traditional physical activity.

Value Proposition

  • Target market: aging populations, neurological disease patients, mobility-impaired individuals
  • Scientific foundation: decades of exercise neuroscience research
  • Therapeutic candidates: BDNF, IGF-1, irisin, cathepsin B, and other exerkines
  • Applications: cognitive enhancement, neuroprotection, metabolic health
  • Competitive advantage: focus on combinatorial approaches mimicking exercise physiology
