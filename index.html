I've published in Cell (Impact Factor 64.5), authored a first-author review in CNS Neuroscience & Therapeutics, and am completing my PhD in Behavioral Neuroscience at the University at Albany.

Featured Projects

Meta-Analysis of Environmental Enrichment & Cognition

Doctoral thesis using meta-analytic methods to investigate translational validity of preclinical rodent models. Found rodents show ~2.5× larger cognitive enhancement from enrichment than humans — suggesting standard lab housing may systematically inflate preclinical effect sizes.

Brain-wide GABAergic Interneuron Mapping

Contributing author on a Cell publication (IF 64.5) from Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory. Used serial two-photon tomography to map the distribution of three GABAergic interneuron subtypes across the entire mouse brain at single-cell resolution.
