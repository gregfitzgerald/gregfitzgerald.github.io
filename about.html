About

I'm a researcher focused on neuroscience and cognitive science, with particular interests in meta-analysis and research methodology.

My work centers on:

  • Meta-analysis and systematic review methods
  • Cognitive neuroscience research
  • Science communication and rationality
  • Research methodology and best practices

I write about the intersection of neuroscience, methodology, and effective thinking. My goal is to contribute to more rigorous and reproducible research practices.

Background in statistics, experimental design, and cognitive science. Interested in how we can improve the quality and impact of scientific research.
