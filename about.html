About

I'm a neuroscience researcher focused on meta-analysis, research methodology, and improving scientific practices.

research interests

  • Meta-analysis and systematic review methodology
  • Publication bias and selective reporting
  • Cognitive neuroscience research synthesis
  • Research reproducibility and replication
  • Science communication and rationality

background

I have experience in statistical analysis, experimental design, and evidence synthesis. My work focuses on developing better methods for aggregating research findings and identifying sources of bias in the scientific literature.

I'm particularly interested in how cognitive biases affect research practices and how we can use systematic approaches to improve the quality and reliability of scientific evidence.

writing

I write regularly about research methodology, science communication, and rationality. You can find my posts on Substack and occasional contributions to LessWrong and the EA Forum.

  • Academic productivity and workflow optimization
